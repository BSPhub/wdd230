Target Audience

This site is meant to attract both current and future business owners that are looking to both network within the community as well as help to grow Nampa into the vision we have for the city. Displaying the unique character of this city is a key component in attracting business to Nampa.

Personas

Madeleine Baker is a twenty two year old recent graduate from UCR Riverside, CA. She is in Graphic Design/Marketing and is looking to establish a working studio in a downtown location that is located in a financial district. Her hope is that she can network with other working Tech professionals and build a quality team within her first year.

Franklin Doyle owns a custom Motorcycle shop in Arizona and is looking to relocate to be closer to family in Idaho. Franklin is fifty one years old and has established his brand in his field, but is looking to establish new contacts for his shop opening near the center of Nampa. Franklin was very involved in local outreach programs in Arizona and looks to do the same in Idaho.

Scenarios

  • Madeleine is looking to find other Graphic Designers in the Nampa area that are looking to share her vision for her studio.
  • Franklin is looking to get involved in the community helping out through events and outreach programs
  • users may want to contact members of the chamber of commerce for a directory of local businesses
  • users may want to contact the chamber of commerce to get information on local events that businesses are hosting
  • users may want to contact the site to get an idea of what attractions the city has for residents and visitors
